Full Job Description

About the Project: Pluto Train 2 is the expansion of a second LNG train at the existing Pluto LNG onshore facility near Karratha in Western Australia run by Woodside Energy. It will process Scarborough gas and have an LNG capacity of ~5 million tonnes per annum.

Responsibilities:

  • Read and interpret Isometric drawings
  • Site measure and fit large bore and small bore pipe
  • Work to Bechtel piping procedures and tolerances
  • Installation of pipe supports
  • Complete Flange Management and ITR's
  • Work with supervision and engineering to complete IWP's

Qualifications and Skills:

  • Minimum of 3 years demonstrated experience in a similar position within the construction & LNG industry
  • Experience reading isometric drawings and use of P&IDs
  • Previous experience using clamshell and oxy cutting
  • Australian recognised fabrication or fitting trade certificate
  • White Card
  • Ability to pass pre-employment medical, drug & alcohol screening
  • Previous Bechtel experience is desirable

What's on Offer:

  • 3:1 roster, FIFO from Perth. Direct flights available from Brisbane to Karratha
  • 68 hour work week
  • Fly home morning of day 21, paid at ordinary hours - home the same day!
  • RDO is accrued - choose to have it paid during RNR or at the end of your contract
  • Full time, permanent employment (5 weeks of annual leave, 10 days of personal leave)
  • $47.30 per hour base rate
  • $1.88 per hour tradesperson allowance (bringing base to $49.18 + penalties)
  • $5.40 per hour site allowance
  • $175 per week project incentive payment (PIP)
  • Severance payment of 2 x base hourly rate per week
  • Weekly pay

Additional Information: This position involves exposure to changing environmental and construction conditions. The Pluto Train 2 Project is a Major Hazard Facility with specific site restrictions.
